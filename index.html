ABOUT THE STUDIO

I sculpt digital experiences that take small brands seriously.

Yugh Studio is run by Yugesh, a designer and developer based in Karachi. I build websites the way a good chef cooks. Carefully, with intention, and always with the end customer in mind.

How I work

Four principles that guide every project.

auto_awesome

Detail over decoration

Typography, spacing, micro-interactions, mobile experience. The little things most designers skip are the things that make a site feel finished.

diamond

Built to convert

A beautiful site that doesn't get clicks is wallpaper. I design for action first, looks second.

hub

Mobile first, always

Most of your customers will visit on phone. Your site should look incredible there, not just on a desktop.

Yours to keep

No vendor lock-in, no subscription traps. The website I build is yours, hosted wherever you want, editable however you want.
